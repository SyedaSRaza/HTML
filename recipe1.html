Oreo Milkshake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pint vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 8 Oreo cookies
  • 1 tbsp chocolate sauce

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients for milkshake
  2. Place the ice cream, milk, 8 of the Oreo cookies, and the chocolate sauce in a blender, and puree until smooth.
  3. Crush the remaining Oreo cookies by placing them in a zipper-sealed plastic bag, and pounding on them a few times with a rolling pin until they crumble.
  4. Pour the milkshakes into two glasses, and top each with crushed Oreo cookie crumbs.
  5. Serve the shakes immediately, with a straw, and enjoy!
